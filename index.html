About me

I am Theo, a developer focused on creating modern, efficient, and scalable solutions that combine creativity with functionality. My journey began with 3D modeling for games, where I developed a strong sense of design and attention to detail, but it was in programming that I found my true passion. Today, I build responsive and visually engaging web interfaces using HTML and CSS, and I use JavaScript to add interactivity and dynamic behavior to applications. I also have experience with Lua, especially in game development and scripting environments, which allows me to create flexible and optimized solutions.In addition to front-end development, I am continuously evolving in backend technologies, particularly building APIs with Python and working with databases using SQL. I also have experience managing local database environments using XAMPP. Furthermore, I enjoy exploring game development with Godot; although I am still a beginner, I am very interested in learning and creating projects within the engine. I also work with automation processes, always aiming to optimize workflows and improve efficiency by reducing repetitive tasks. I am constantly learning and improving, with the goal of developing impactful solutions that balance performance, usability, and innovation.
